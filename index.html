About Me

Hello, my name is Belen Cuevas (she/her), and I am an emerging professional furthering my education in Application Development at Year Up Puget Sound and Contemporary Business at Renton Technical College. Through Year Up, I am learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am a member of our Student Ambassadors program. Through RTC, I am learning the fundamentals of creating and/or managing a business.

As a first-generation Latina woman breaking into STEM, I want to inspire women and Latinos to break walls down and achieve career roles that aren't usually advertised for us. My cultural and familial experiences instilled in me a strong work ethic and a sense of dedication. I have worked in a small business restaurant and big corporate groceries stores, where I was able to connect with many diverse people. The communication skills I gained from my previous jobs have led me to discover my passion for the evolving topics of business, technology, and finances.

My values include spending time with my family and friends and ensuring I live a healthy life so I can be successful. I love to expand my knowledge by discovering new books and take on new challenges.
